What is una?

una is a mobile app that makes planning small, informal meetups simple and stress-free. Where group chats often get messy and existing tools feel too formal, una keeps it light, intuitive, and spontaneous.

Built on research with young adults, una focuses on three core features: Drop-in , Today or Never and Based on Mood.

una proves that digital tools don’t have to be heavy or overwhelming they can support real, spontaneous, and meaningful moments with friends.

Why una?

Drop in

Let your friends know you’re free right now. No need to plan days ahead.

How it works

Share your location → friends see it instantly → if they’re free too, they can join right away.

Today or Never

Events that happen today if it doesn’t work out, no worries.

How it works

Create a one-day invite → friends have until midnight to join → no stress if it doesn’t happen.

Mood-based Invites

Choose your vibe and una suggests a fitting hangout.

How it works

Select your mood (chill, active, fun) → una generates a suggested activity → invite a friend instantly.
